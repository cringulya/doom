** Font

** Evil tweaks
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
;; restore s/S vim behaviour
(remove-hook 'doom-first-input-hook #'evil-snipe-mode)

;; enable clipboard
(setq-default evil-clipboard t)
#+end_src
